RPG (Rex Preferred Guest) is a frequent patronage program designed to reward guests for staying at THE REX HOTEL SAIGON. RPG members should familiarize themselves with the following Terms and Conditions, which are subject to change from time to time. Should you require further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact our RPG Membership Services at: (84) 8 3822 8387 or rexpreferredguest@rex.com.vn

Membership Eligibility

+ To become our Silver member. You need to have 03 qualifying room nights per year at Rex hotel.

+ To become our Gold member. You need to earn 3000 accumulation points ~ US$3000 room nights spent per year at Rex hotel.

+ To become our Platinum member. You need to earn 6000 accumulation points ~ US$6000 room nights spent per year at Rex hotel.

1. All individual travelers are eligible for RPG membership. RPG membership is not transferable.

2. Membership in RPG is open only to individual residents in countries where the program is permitted by law.

3. Membership in RPG is valid within 1 year

4. The Hotel reserves the right to refuse Membership without a given reason or reject any application if the required information is not supplied accurately and in full.
